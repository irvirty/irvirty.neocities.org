Curiosity is a Mars rover that is exploring Gale crater and Mount Sharp on Mars as part of NASA's Mars Science Laboratory (MSL) mission.[2] Launched in 2011 and landed the following year, the car-sized rover continues to operate more than a decade after its original two-year mission.
